open source statistics shipper for Postgresql

  • Documentation

Log Metrics to CSV

Pgsampler is a background worker. It starts and stops with your server, and can be configured to log a history of performance statistics in flat files for later analysis.

Ship Metrics to Pgantenna

Pgsampler can be configured to send statistics to a pgantenna instance, storing the data in an containerized Postgresql cluster just for monitoring, alerting, and analysis.
